About the game

Hook the stars.
Swing through the cosmos.
Ascend.

Aibek is a momentum-driven grapple-swinging platformer set among drifting stars and endless sky. Launch from a floating platform, latch onto stars with a single tap, and carry your swing into the next leap. Every run is a chase for altitude — read the arc, time the release, and climb higher than the last attempt.

Grapple & Swing

Standard stars power your momentum but burn out while you're hooked — hold on too long and they fizzle. Release at the apex to convert swing into distance.

Immune Hooks

Cyan stars never decay, anchoring safe havens when the sky gets thin. Chain them with shooting stars for risky, high-score trajectories.

Moon Power

Charge the moon bar as you ascend, then unleash it for a burst of lift. A built-in sandbox lets you sketch your own constellations and share the climb.

Standard star
01 · Grapple & swing

Every hook is a fuse.

Tap a standard star and its outer ring ignites as you swing. The longer you hang on, the lower it burns — from green, to amber, to an angry red. Hold past empty and the star collapses, dropping you mid-arc.

The art is in the release: let go at the apex to turn swing into altitude before the ring runs out.

DepletesWhile grappled
RewardMomentum & lift
RiskSupernova collapse
Immune star
02 · Immune hooks

Anchors that don't burn.

Cyan stars never deplete. Swing as long as you need, line up the next leap, and let go on your own terms. They're the safe havens between risk — the staging posts between high-score trajectories.

Chain them with standards and shooting stars when the sky gets thin.

DepletesNever
UseSet up the next jump
TempoYour call
Shooting star
03 · Shooting stars

Hook a moving target.

Yellow stars streak diagonally across a region, dragging you along for the ride. The ring drains as they travel — connect late and there's less arc to work with before the star fades and throws you off.

Time it right and a shooting star is a slingshot. Time it wrong and you're airborne with no handhold.

DepletesAs it travels
RewardHuge lateral reach
SkillRead the path
